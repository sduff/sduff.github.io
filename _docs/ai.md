I have always had a fascination for Artificial Intelligence, the ability for
a machine to behave in an intelligent manner. I've been involved in many AI
related projects, both formal and informal. Here is a very short list.

## RoboCup

I was a member of a joint RMIT/Melbourne University RoboCup Legged League team
in 2000.  

That same year, I also developed a class for High School students to
learn some [basics of Artificial Intelligence]
(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subsumption_architecture), embodying them in Lego
Robots that also played Soccer.

The University Computer Science Newsletter covered the story.


## Proactive Maintenance Goals

I commenced more formal research in Artificial Intelligence in 2005, concluding
with a thesis on _[Proactive Maintenance Goals](pmg)_.

## Machine Learning

Since then, my interests in AI have more focused on applications of Artificial
Intelligence, in particular Machine Learning for [Information
Security](security).
