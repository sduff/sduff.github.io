Historical

It turns out, I wasn't the first to combine memory and four-in-a-row. In 1989, Parker Brothers released "In The Dark", which involved a hidden four-in-a-row field. When one player believed they had won, the markers were revealed to show if they were correct or not. In 1992, a German publisher Piatnik released "4 Versteckt" which had the same premise.
